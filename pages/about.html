About

The first chapter of Italo Calvino's If on a winter's night a traveler, a lovely meditation on reading, describes many types of books. I first read this list in September 2013 and "Inexplicable Curiosity, Not Easily Justified" has been lodged deeply into my brain ever since. If nothing else, this site is a collection of curiosities, without offer or expectation of justification.
